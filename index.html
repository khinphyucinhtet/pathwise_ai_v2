        Introduction

        Many youth today face high levels of stress during job searching and career development. They often feel unprepared for interviews, unsure about their career direction, and overwhelmed by expectations.

        This project introduces an AI-powered Career & Wellbeing Companion Platform that supports users through interview preparation, resume building, and personalized career guidance while considering mental wellbeing.

        Problem Statement

        Many students and job seekers experience stress, anxiety, and confusion during career development and job searching.

        • Lack of real interview practice and guidance
        • Difficulty creating strong resumes
        • Unclear career direction based on skills
        • Pressure from competition and expectations
        • No personalized system that supports both career growth and mental wellbeing

        This leads to low confidence, poor performance in interviews, and inefficient job searching.

        Objectives

        • To reduce stress in job seeking through AI-guided support
        • To provide interactive interview coaching with real-time feedback
        • To assist users in building professional resumes using AI
        • To recommend suitable careers based on skills and user profile

        AI Solutions

        PathWise AI combines practical career development with wellbeing support so users can build confidence, reduce confusion, and make better career decisions.

        1

        AI Interview Coaching System

        A video-call-like mock interview where users answer realistic questions by voice or text and receive instant feedback.

        • HR AI asks behavioral and personality questions
        • Technical AI asks skill-based and problem-solving questions
        • Manager AI asks decision-making and real-world questions
        • System evaluates confidence, clarity, relevance, and answer quality
        2

        AI Career & Resume System

        Users build a professional CV, explore suitable roles, and get guidance that also considers stress level and work preferences.

        • Resume Builder AI with role-specific suggestions
        • Career Recommendation based on skills, interests, and profile
        • Mental Support Element for balanced and realistic career growth
        Builds confidence Prepares users for interviews with structured role-based practice
        Reduces confusion Connects user profile, wellbeing, and career direction in one platform
        Improves employability Supports resume quality, interview readiness, and career discovery
